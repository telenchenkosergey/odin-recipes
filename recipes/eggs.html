Boursin Baked Eggs

Description

These creamy Boursin baked eggs are super easy to make. They are delicious—superb for a quick warm breakfast or lunch.

Ingredients

  • cooking spray
  • 1 (5.3 ounce) package Boursin® Garlic and Fine Herbs cheese
  • 8 large eggs
  • salt and freshly ground black pepper to taste

Steps

  1. Preheat the oven to 400 degrees F (180 degrees C). Spray a 9x9 baking dish with cooking spray.
  2. Place block of Boursin in the center of the baking dish.
  3. Crack 8 eggs carefully into the baking dish around the block of Boursin.
  4. Bake in the preheated oven until the egg white around the block of Boursin is cooked through, about 15 minutes. Yolks may be slightly runny.
  5. Using a fork, mash the eggs and Boursin together until until well combined. Season to taste with salt and pepper.
